Here are the basics which I learnt in HTML:

1)The 'h' tag represents the heading tag. There are 6 types of heading in HTML as shown below -

h1-Gaurav

h2-Gaurav

h3-Gaurav

h4-Gaurav

h5-Gaurav
h6-Gaurav

2)The 'p' tag represents the paragraph tag, helping us to write a paragraph. We can use a keyword 'Lorem' if we want some dummy texts in our paragraph. E.g- 'Lorem20' This will give a paragraph of 20 dummy words.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur, adipisicing elit. Voluptate sequi, omnis consectetur neque rerum numquam dignissimos. Nostrum quia enim hic.

3)The 'strong' or 'b' tag helps in writing a group of text in bold. E.g- My name is Gaurav Kumar Choudhary .

4)The 'em' or 'i' tag helps in writing a group of texts in ITALICS. E.g- My name is Gaurav Kumar Choudhary.

5)The 'br' tag breaks a line.

6)The 'hr' tag provides a horizontal line.
